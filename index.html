Project Overview

A farm with
two purposes

Business Model

Two pillars.
One ecosystem.

The two pillars are not parallel — they are interdependent. The land feeds the dinners. The dinners demonstrate the value of the land.

1
Pillar 1

Organic Production & Local Commerce

The farm produces micro greens, citrus, aromatic herbs, and seasonal foods. Surplus is sold through local channels across Tavira and the Algarve. Organic certification will formalise practices already underway and open access to markets, restaurants, and distributors that require verified provenance.

Organic Certification · In Progress
2
Pillar 2

Dragão Invites — Farm-to-Table Dinners

Two private dinners per month, for groups of 12. Each dinner is developed by a guest chef who works as much as possible with what the farm produces at that moment. The menu is never set in advance — it is built from what the land offers that day.

Guest Chef · Seasonal Menu

Project Goals

What this project
sets out to achieve

01
Organic Certification
Obtain organic production certification, formalising the agricultural practices already underway at Quinta Feliz Dragão and unlocking access to certified supply channels.
02
Sustainable Local Distribution
Build a reliable local channel for certified organic surplus, reaching markets, restaurants, and partners who require verified provenance and traceability.
03
Grow Dragão Invites
Establish the private dinner programme as a consistent complementary activity that directly valorises organic produce and generates a second revenue stream from the same estate.
04
Regional Reference
Position Quinta Feliz Dragão as a benchmark for organic farming in the Tavira region, contributing to the development of sustainable agriculture in the Algarve.
05
An Integrated, Replicable Model
Create a model in which organic agriculture and gastronomy sustain and communicate each other — the farm supplies the ingredients, the dinners prove their worth.

Why Here

The region has growing demand for local organic produce and for authentic gastronomic experiences rooted in the land. Quinta Feliz Dragão answers both trends simultaneously — and from the same estate.
